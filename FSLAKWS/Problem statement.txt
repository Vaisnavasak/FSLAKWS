#PROBLEM STATEMENT:

Finding a specific word in a long text manually might be easier. 
But looking for a specific word and finding it in a long audio 
manually is a difficult and time consuming process. Basically 
every existing system needs 1000s of word samples and works 
only in a specific language.

#SOLUTION:

The need is to create a proposed solution that takes very few 
samples of the user defined keyword. Then, it looks for that 
keyword in the audio and it presents that word along with the 
timestamp where it was spoken.
